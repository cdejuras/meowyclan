# RoomieOS — Low Level Technical Requirements

## Product Overview

RoomieOS is a roommate management platform designed to reduce friction in shared living environments through automation, transparency, and AI-assisted coordination.

The system centralizes:

* shared expenses
* chores
* groceries
* maintenance
* communication
* fairness analytics

The platform uses AI to:

* balance chores fairly
* generate conflict resolution suggestions
* identify unhealthy contribution patterns
* automate reminders

---

# 1. Product Goals

## Primary Goals

* Reduce roommate conflicts
* Improve payment accountability
* Simplify household management
* Increase transparency in shared responsibilities

## Secondary Goals

* Improve communication
* Reduce emotional tension
* Provide measurable fairness insights

# 35. Final Engineering Recommendations

## Prioritize Simplicity

Avoid premature microservices.

## Strong Domain Boundaries

Keep modules isolated.

## AI as Enhancement

Core features must still function without AI.

## Maintain Auditability

Track:

* expense changes
* assignment history
* fairness calculations

## Avoid Hidden Algorithms

Fairness calculations should remain transparent to users.
